Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a popular North Indian dish made with tender pieces of chicken cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-based gravy. It was first created in Delhi and has become one of the most famous Indian dishes worldwide.

The chicken is usually marinated in yogurt, lemon juice, and spices such as garam masala, turmeric, cumin, and chili powder. It is then grilled or roasted before being added to the buttery tomato sauce.

The sauce is made with tomatoes, butter, cream, ginger, garlic, and aromatic spices. The result is a mildly spicy, slightly sweet, and very creamy curry.

Butter Chicken is typically served with naan, roti, or basmati rice. It is loved for its smooth texture, rich flavor, and comforting taste.
